#+title: Monad

- tags :: [[file:20200929171259-endofunctor.org][Endofunctor]], [[file:20200927165017-natural_transformation.org][Natural Transformation]]

- A monad over a category $C$ is a triple $(T,\eta ,\mu)$
- $T$ :: [[file:20200929171259-endofunctor.org][Endofunctor]] on $C$
- $\eta : Id_C \Rightarrow T$
- $\mu : T^2 \Rightarrow T$

Such that this diagrams commute:
[[./imgs/monaddefcommute.png]]

- $\eta$ is the /unit/
- $\mu$ is the /multiplication/
- Both are [[file:20200927165017-natural_transformation.org][natural transformations]]
