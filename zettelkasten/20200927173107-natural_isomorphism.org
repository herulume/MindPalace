#+title: Natural Isomorphism

- tags :: [[file:20200927165017-natural_transformation.org][Natural Transformation]], [[file:20200916190424-isomorphism.org][Isomorphism]]

If each component of a [[file:20200927165017-natural_transformation.org][natural transformation]] $\eta$ is an [[file:20200916190424-isomorphism.org][isomorphism]]
then $\eta$ is a natural isomorphism

$\eta : F \xrightarrow{\cong} G$
